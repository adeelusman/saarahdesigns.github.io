About Me


My name is Saarah Aniz Chachia, I’m 21, born on September 22nd 1997 in Croydon, UK. I’m British, my parents are born and raised in Malawi, Africa and moved to the UK when they were young and my siblings and I were born there. We moved to Malaysia when I was 2 so you could say I’m practically Malaysian. I aspire to become a journalist one day, more specifically a travel journalist because I’d like to see as much of the world as I can and at the same time write about it.
